Interests

Almost every evening (and sometimes during the day if there is time) I draw in my sketchbook or, more recently with my iPad and Apple Pencil. I have always loved drawing and greatly enjoy exercising my creativity whenever possible. I also love to make music and have a knack for learning instruments. My favourites are acoustic guitar and piano which I have played for many years. I have my eye on accordion as my next musical conquest. I enjoy being outside and when time and finances allow, I like to engage in more adventurous activities such as skiing, shooting, mountain-bike riding and scuba.
